    Day 1

    Match Play Team Better Ball 2 vs 2 - Best scoring ball from each team is counted towards the teams score for that hole, whichever team has the lowest score wins a point, a drawn hole is 0.5 each. Each 9 holes will count as an independant match, meaning each group has 2 points to play for over 18.

    Day 2

    Match Play Head 2 Head & Single Championship - 4 individual match ups to be decided by the teams. Each match follows the same format as Day 1 regarding scoring and matches. All points accrued are added together to decide which team wins the cup. If its a draw after 36 holes we go to a playoff - each player in the team takes it in turn to have a shot, this continues until 1 team wins a hole. Each player will be also competing for the singles trophy with the lowest scoring round with handicaps taken into consideration wins and organises next years trip.

    Handicaps & Mullies

    Day 1 - Handicaps to be discussed in person. Each player gets 1 mulligan per 18
    Day 2 - Handicaps decided as a culmination equation of general playing handicap & Day 1's performance. Each player gets 1 mulligan per 18
